Amazing Minestrone

Description

This is the most amazing minestrone soup you will ever taste. Honestly. It's thick and full of vegetable goodness. The secret is the blended beans and plenty of hearty veggies and Italian spices.

You will not believe how good this makes your house smell.

Insert a long anecdote about making this soup and how it changed my life.

Ingredients

  • One can of white beans
  • Broth, 32 oz
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • onions
  • carrots
  • garlic
  • celery
  • parmesan rind or some grated
  • basil
  • oregano
  • rosemary
  • bay leaf
  • small pasta, 2 cups
  • zucchini, chopped small

Steps

  1. Blend the beans with about a cup of broth
  2. In a big soup pot, heat the onions, celery, garlic and carrots about 5 min
  3. Add tomatoes
  4. Add beans
  5. Add broth, veggies, spices and pasta
  6. Simmer about 40 minutes
  7. Add the zucchini and simmer about 10 more minutes
  8. Enjoy!
